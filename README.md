<h1>🌍 Global Superstore Analysis Dashboard</h1>
<h3>🚀 End-to-End Data Analysis Project (Python → SQL → Power BI)</h3>
<h2>📖 Project Overview</h2>

This project delivers a complete end-to-end data analysis solution using the Global Superstore dataset.

<h4>The objective is to analyze:</h4>

• Sales performance
<br>
• Profitability drivers
<br>
• Customer behavior across regions

<h4>The project integrates:</h4>


• Data cleaning (Python)
<br>
• Business analysis (SQL)
<br>
• Interactive dashboard (Power BI)
<br>
💡 The goal is to transform raw data into actionable business insights.

<h3>🎯 Problem Statement</h3>
• Analyze sales, profit, and customer behavior across categories and regions
<br>
• Identify key issues such as low-performing regions and high discount impact
<br>
• Provide data-driven recommendations to improve business performance

<h3>🔍 Key Insights</h3>
<h4>📊 Sales Insights</h4>
• Technology category generates the highest sales
<br>
• Central region is the top revenue contributor
<br>
• Sales show steady growth (2011–2014)
<h3>💰 Profit Insights</h3>
• High discounts significantly reduce profitability
<br>
• Some products consistently generate losses
<br>
• Technology category is the most profitable

<h4>👥 Customer Insights</h4>
• Revenue is concentrated among top customers
<br>
&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;&nbsp;• e.g., Tom Ashbrook, Tamara Chand
<br>
• Consumer segment contributes ~51.48% of total sales

<h3>💡 Business Recommendations</h3>
• 📉 Optimize pricing strategy by reducing excessive discounts
<br>
• 📦 Focus on high-margin categories (Technology, Office Supplies)
<br>

• 👥 Implement customer loyalty programs
<br>
•🌍 Improve performance in low-performing regions (Canada, EMEA)
